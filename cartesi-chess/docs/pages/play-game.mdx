# How to play Chess.mef

MEF Chess is an on-chain chess platform where you can play the chess game with your friends and build an on-chain reputation

## Steps to play the Game

1. Go to [Chess.mef](https://cartesi-chess.vercel.app/)
2. Authenticate your wallet address using Sign in with Ethereum
3. Deposit 1200 MEF token to the Dapp Portal 
4. Then click on [Play Game](https://cartesi-chess.vercel.app/play) 
5. Copy the url for the game and your opponent must have followed the first four processes to be able to join the game
